TOKYO

Welcome to Tokyo, the vibrant and pulsating heart of Japan, which delights and amazes. Tokyo is one of the cities that combines history and modern technology.

Shopping in Harajuku and Akihabara

If you're interested in youth fashion, visiting Harajuku is a must as it's the epicenter of street fashion in Japan.

Akihabara, on the other hand, is the center of entertainment with its bright lights and various activities. The area also boasts many cafes and restaurants offering a variety of delicacies.

A temple older than Tokyo itself

In Tokyo, you can find a temple older than itself, Senso-ji. The area is particularly lively and colorful due to the vendors and craftsmen on the streets surrounding the temple.

Senso-ji is also especially popular during the hanami season when Japanese locals and tourists gather to admire the cherry blossoms blooming around the temple grounds."

Tokyo National Museum

If you plan to visit a museum, the Tokyo National Museum is a sure and top choice. The museum features ceramics, Buddhist sculptures, samurai swords, Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock prints, kimonos, and much more.
