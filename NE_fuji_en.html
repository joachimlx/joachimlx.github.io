FUJI

Welcome to Mount Fuji, Japan's famous active volcano. Mount Fuji is the most iconic attraction in Japan known worldwide, and many come to climb it, but its beauty can also be admired from afar.

Climbing Mount Fuji

Climbing Mount Fuji is a unique experience that offers an incredible opportunity to experience Japan's stunning nature and landscapes. The climbing season on Mount Fuji is typically from July to August when weather conditions are most favorable and reaching the summit is easiest.

Many climbers opt for day climbing, starting late in the afternoon so they arrive at the summit for sunrise and can admire the stunning view. Others prefer night climbing, starting early in the evening and arriving at the summit at sunrise.
